# 📈 Talitha Félix

**`Data driven (Data scientist/Data analyst/System analyst) `**

👋 Hello! I'm a Data Scientist with a strong foundation in machine learning, data analysis, and visualization. With a background in Systems Development and an MBA in Data Science & AI, I am passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights.

🚀 My areas of expertise include:

📊 Data wrangling, cleaning, and visualization using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib
🤖 Machine Learning and AI, focusing on regression analysis, classification, and deep learning
🏇 Applications of Geometric Morphometrics and AI in morphological studies and performance analysis
🛠 Experience in DevOps and data science pipelines
💡 I'm always exploring new tools and technologies to enhance data-driven decision-making, and I love collaborating on impactful projects!

Feel free to check out my repositories and get in touch if you're interested in discussing data science, AI, or new opportunities!
